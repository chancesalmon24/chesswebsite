About Your Instructor

♛

With over 15 years of chess teaching experience, I'm passionate about sharing the beauty and strategy of chess with students of all ages. I hold a USCF rating of 2100 and have coached numerous students to tournament success.

My teaching philosophy focuses on making chess fun and accessible while building strong fundamentals. Whether you're a complete beginner or looking to break through to the next level, I'll tailor lessons to your specific needs and goals.

Qualifications:
  • USCF Certified Chess Coach
  • 15+ years teaching experience
  • Tournament director certification
  • Youth chess program coordinator
